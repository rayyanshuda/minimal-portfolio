I find myself
Rewriting her outline 
In my mind
Every night

The kind of silence
She leaves behind
Feels like a lifetime
Carved into my side

I tried to define
What it is about her eyes
That makes the light
Feel less like light

And more like a sign
That I was designed
To find her
In every lifetime

She's the fine line
Between alive
And just
Surviving

I'd cross the divide
Climb through the night
Just to remind her
She's the reason I try
