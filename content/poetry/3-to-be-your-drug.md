You shot it up your veins 
It mixed in and became you 
You rolled your eyes back 
And I wished I could know you that well

It travels where I never could 
Through every part of you 
Touching things you never let me touch 
Knowing rooms in you 
I was never shown the door to

You let it in without a fight 
But me
I knocked for years

It doesn't even love you 
And yet it lives inside your chest 
Where I was never allowed to rest

I watched it move across your face 
Rearrange you 
Make you someone softer 
Someone I almost recognized

And I hated it 
Not because it was killing you 
But because you trusted it 

More than you ever trusted me

And all I ever wanted 
Was one room 
One honest corner of you 
That you didn't lock away
