when i wake up i want to hear the leaves rustling with the soft breeze. i want my room to dimly light up from the sunlight passing through my windows. i want to feel the warmth of the sun on my face to remind myself that i can feel things so mundane, yet so beautiful. i want to put on the most joyous songs and sway myself without a care in the world. i want to see the curve of my mother's smile. i want to hear the faint laughter of strangers having a good day outside. i want to see the sun set and the moon rise and see the sky colour itself in the softest colours in between. i want to feel the cold wind at night and count the stars until i get bored. i want to sing my old, favorite songs until the faded nostalgia takes over me. a version of my happy.
