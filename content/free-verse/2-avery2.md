i long for what they call the bad weather. the one where the lightning brightens your dark room for half a second, and then the loud thunder follows, shaking your core, reminding you of the weight you carry within yourself. when the pounding raindrops fall, you can feel the dampness in the air, and all the lost memories come back rushing in an instant. and then you struggle, thinking whether you should let the melancholy engulf you in your bed, or let the wind, carrying flickers of the rain, push you into the abyss of half forgotten dreams that never reached an end.
